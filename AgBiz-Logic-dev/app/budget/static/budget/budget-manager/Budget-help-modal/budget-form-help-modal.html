Budget Manager Help

To view or edit an existing budget, simply click on the View/Edit button to the right of the budget in your Budget Manager list. Make the desired edits to the budget and click Save. To delete a budget from your account, click on the Remove button to the right of the budget and then click either Confirm or Cancel to complete the action. To print, click on the PDF button.

If you have a very long list of budgets, you can search the list by clicking on the Search for a Budget link or the Search Budgets button. A Search screen appears, allowing you to filter by title or keyword with the option of selecting enterprise, state and county. Select the desired budget from the filtered list and click Edit to open the budget.

To create a new budget from an existing budget in your account, click the Copy Existing Budget button. The Search screen appears, allowing you to filter and select the budget you want to copy. Give the new budget a unique name, then click the Copy Budget button. The newly created budget will display in your list next to the original, ready to edit as needed.

To add a University or Industry Budget from the ABL Library, click on the Use ABL Library Budget button. The Search screen pops up, allowing you to find and select the pre-built budget you want to add to your account. Select the desired budget then click the Copy and Edit button to open it. Click Save and the ABL Library Budget will be added to your Budget Manager list.

To create a new budget from scratch, click on the Create New Budget button. Enter a name for the budget and select an enterprise, enterprise type, commodity, class and market, then click the Add New Budget button. A new, empty budget displays, where you can enter Income and Costs before clicking Save to add the new budget to your list.

Budget Manager allows you to combine two or more existing budgets to create a new one. Click on the Combine Existing Budgets button and select the first budget you want to combine. Click on the Add button, and the selected budget appears in the Budgets Selected window. Select a second budget from the Search screen and again click Add. At this point you can either add more budgets or click the Combine Selected Budgets button. The newly created combined budget displays, where you can make any desired edits such as modifying the budget name or removing duplicate cost items that you only pay once during the season associated with the budget. When you have finished editing the combined budget, click Save and the budget will be added to your list.
